To Mireia

I wanted you to wake up today with a smile, because today is special — it’s been one month since we officially became “us.” And even though I can’t be there to kiss you good morning, I hope these words feel like a warm hug from me to you. The truth is, my love for you began long before that day. From the very first time I saw your smile, I knew something in me had changed. Your beauty leaves me speechless… You have eyes that shine like honey in the sunlight, lips that always feel like a promise, and skin as soft as your voice when you speak gently. Your long, dark hair falls like a hypnotic waterfall, and your smile… that smile is my favorite place in the world. But what I love most about you is what can’t be seen. You’re patient, understanding, affectionate, with a brilliant mind that inspires me every day. You’re funny, sweet, thoughtful… You’re everything I didn’t even know I was looking for. Distance keeps me from holding you today, but my love doesn’t care about distance. Because I carry you in every thought, every breath, every “I miss you.” One month with you, and I already can’t imagine life without you. Thank you for being exactly who you are. Thank you for making me so happy. I hope your morning is as beautiful as your heart. I love you — happy one month, mi amor.

De Jonny
